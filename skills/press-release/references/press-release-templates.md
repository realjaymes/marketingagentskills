## Newsworthiness Scoring

Rate 1-10 on each factor:

| Factor | Questions |
|--------|-----------|
| Timeliness | Is this happening now? Is there urgency? |
| Relevance | Does target audience care? |
| Impact | How significant is the change? |
| Specificity | Are there concrete details and numbers? |
| Proof | Is there verifiable evidence? |

**Score Interpretation:**
- 8-10: Strong news, proceed with confidence
- 6-7: Decent news, consider strengthening
- Below 6: Weak news, suggest revisions or frame as update

| Angle | When to Use |
|-------|-------------|
| Product Innovation | New technology, unique approach |
| Market Trend | Timing with industry movement |
| Customer Outcome | Strong results data |
| Industry Shift | Broader implications |
| Regulatory Context | Compliance, policy relevance |

## Headline Formulas

1. **News-led**: [Company] Launches [Product] to [Solve Problem]
2. **Results-led**: [Company] Announces [Metric] Growth with [Product]
3. **Trend-led**: [Industry Trend] Drives [Company] to Launch [Product]

## FAQ Categories

1. **Product/Feature Questions**: What does it do? How does it work?
2. **Availability Questions**: When? Where? How much?
3. **Comparison Questions**: How is this different?
4. **Technical Questions**: Requirements, integrations
5. **Company Questions**: Who, why, background
