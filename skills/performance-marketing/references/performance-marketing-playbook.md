What an Ad Is (and What It Is Not)

An ad is **not** a salesperson. An ad is a filter.

Its job is to:

> â— Qualify the right audience
>
> â— Move the right person one step forward

Its job is **not** to:

> â— Explain everything â— Convince skeptics
>
> â— Close complex purchases

Most ads fail because they attempt to compress the entire buyer journey
into a single impression. High-performing ads respect cognitive limits
