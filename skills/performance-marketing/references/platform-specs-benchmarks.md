# Platform Specs & KPI Benchmarks

Quick-reference for ad format specifications and performance benchmarks across Meta, TikTok, Google, LinkedIn, Snapchat, X, and ChatGPT / AI Answer Engine Ads.

---

### ChatGPT / AI Answer Engine Ads

| Surface / Format | Specs | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **ChatGPT Sponsored Recommendation Card** | Text only; Headline 50-80 chars / Description 150-200 chars / Proof element 80-120 chars | Single unit type as of June 2026; no image, video, or carousel formats live yet |
| **Microsoft Copilot** (via Microsoft Ads) | Inherits Microsoft Ads RSA (15 headlines/30 chars, 4 descriptions/90 chars), Audience Network, Shopping, PMax specs | Copilot inventory included automatically when syndicated search partners enabled |
| **Google AI Overviews** (via Google Ads) | Inherits Google Ads RSA, Shopping, PMax, Demand Gen specs | AIO renders existing Search/Shopping/PMax campaigns alongside AI answers |

**ChatGPT Ads creative constraints:** Must be visually distinguishable from ChatGPT itself (no chatbot-mimicking voice). Destination consistent with ad promise (no bait-and-switch &mdash; OpenAI rejects). Disallowed at launch: dating, sexual, health claims, alcohol/drugs, healthcare, financial services, legal services, gambling, political. Allowed: lifestyle/household, local services, travel, digital products &amp; education.

### ChatGPT / AI Answer Engine Ads

ChatGPT Ads proper (US/CA/AU/NZ-targeted only as of June 2026):

| Metric | Range / Floor |
|--------|---------------|
| CPM (default floor) | $60 |
| CPM (reported by early advertisers) | $25-$60 |
| CPC (recommended starting bid) | $3-$5 |
| Minimum spend | $0 (no platform minimum post May 5, 2026); practical floor $25-$50/day to read signal |
| CTR in sponsored slot | 2-5%+ healthy |
| CVR (post-click, immediate session) | 3-10%+ (higher than social) |
| Pixel-attributed CPA vs blended (pixel + survey) | Pixel reads ~40% of true conversions; blend in survey + branded search lift |

| CTR (Search) | > 4% | 2-4% | < 2% |
| CPC | < $1.50 | $1.50-3 | > $3 |
| CPM (Audience) | < $10 | $10-15 | > $15 |

Google AI Overviews (via Google Ads) inherits existing Google Ads benchmarks listed above; AIO eligibility is automatic for Search / Shopping / Performance Max campaigns.

**Critical attribution metric on AI surfaces:**

| Metric | Healthy Range | What it tells you |
|---|---|---|
| Pixel CPA / Blended CPA ratio | 0.3-0.5 | Healthy conversation gap signal (~60% of conversions captured outside immediate session via survey + branded search) |
| Survey AI-attribution % (rolling 30d) | Climbing over 30-60 days | Surface is exposing brand effectively |
| Branded search lift (4-week rolling) | Positive trend over 60-90 days | Indirect signal validating AI surface visibility |

### Benchmark Usage Rules

- Benchmarks are directional, not absolute
- Industry, audience, offer, and creative quality all shift ranges
- After 30 days, use client's own historical data as the primary benchmark
- Platform-reported metrics are inflated; cross-reference with analytics
