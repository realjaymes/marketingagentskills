# Lifecycle Marketing Campaign Examples

## SaaS Example: Project Management Tool

### Free Plan User

**Goals:**
- Drive first project creation within 3 days
- Encourage inviting 1-2 team members
- Convert to Basic Plan within 14 days

| Day | Focus | Description | CTA |
|-----|-------|-------------|-----|
| Day 0 | Welcome & Quick Start | Setup guide, ease of creating project | "Create Your First Project" |
| Day 2 | Collaboration Value | Benefits of inviting teammates | "Invite Your Team" |
| Day 5 | Limit Awareness | Showcase limits of free plan | "Upgrade to Basic" |
| Day 8 | Feature Spotlight | Highlight integrations (Slack, Drive) | "Connect Your Tools" |
| Day 12 | Case Study | Small team scaling story | "Upgrade Today" |
| Day 14 | Final Reminder | Urgency, what they lose | "Unlock Full Access" |

### Basic Plan User

**Goals:**
- Consistent use of projects and integrations
- Build habit loops (weekly logins)
- Drive upgrade to Pro when needs arise

| Day | Focus | Description | CTA |
|-----|-------|-------------|-----|
| Day 0 | Welcome to Basic | Highlight unlocked features | "Start a New Project" |
| Day 3 | Productivity Wins | Template packs | "Use a Template" |
| Day 7 | Integrations Spotlight | Slack/Drive connection | "Connect Your Tools" |
| Day 14 | Collaboration Value | Invite more teammates | "Invite Your Team" |
| Day 21 | Feature Teaser (Pro) | Dashboards & automations | "Explore Pro Features" |
| Day 30 | Upgrade Path | Case study Basic to Pro | "Upgrade to Pro" |

### Pro Trial User

**Goals:**
- Drive adoption of advanced features
- Build urgency around trial expiration
- Convert to paying Pro subscribers

| Day | Focus | Description | CTA |
|-----|-------|-------------|-----|
| Day 0 | Welcome to Pro Trial | Dashboards, automations, reporting | "Try Dashboards Now" |
| Day 3 | Workflow Automation | Demo guide | "Set Up Automation" |
| Day 7 | Team Collaboration | Invite teammates | "Invite Your Team" |
| Day 10 | Analytics Value | Tracking productivity | "View Your Reports" |
| Day 12 | Trial Ending Soon | Days left, features at risk | "Upgrade to Pro" |
| Day 14 | Last Chance | Incentive offer | "Upgrade Today" |
