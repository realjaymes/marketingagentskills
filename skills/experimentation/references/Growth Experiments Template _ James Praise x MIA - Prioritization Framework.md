# Growth Experiments Template _ James Praise x MIA - Prioritization Framework

Growth Experiments Tracker Template by James Praise x Marketing In Action
Step Details Impact Index Confidence Index Effort Index
Impact How big of an improvement could this experiment drive? 1: Unknown or minimal 1: Not confident 1: Less than ½ day
Confidence How confident are we in the experiment's success? 2-4: Small, 1-10% relative gain 2: Somewhat confident 2: ½ to 1 day
Efort- Engineering How much engineering time will it require? 5-8: Medium, 10%-25% relative gain 3: Moderately confident 3: 1-2 days
Effort - Marketing What lift is required from the marketing team? 9-10: Large/Huge, +25% relative gain 4: Very confident 4: 2-4 days
Effort - Other Any additional resources needed (e.g., operations, product, etc.) 5: Extremely confident 5: 5-10 days
